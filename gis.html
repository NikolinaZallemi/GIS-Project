Landslides

in Lombardy region

The project is focused on one of the most critical natural hazards in the Lombardy Region, in Northen Italy. Mainly our group analyzed the mountainous area around lake Endine.

The web presents all the steps that were followed for the realization
of the study, as well as statistical analysis related to landslide
susceptibility mapping. This technique helps us to highlight high and
very high hazard zones. Consequently it helps us prepare for the
prevention of future landslide occurrence.
